Notes on Datasets:

FullData_Sept27_2018.csv

Includes data from the following grants:

- A197 (1)
- A182 (2)
- A40-4 (3)
- A195 (4)

Includes the following measures

- SubjectID
- Project (see numbering above)
- age.mri (age at MRI)
- age.beh (age at behavioral testing)
- gender
- sspan.raw (sentence span raw score)
- ppvt.raw (ppvt raw score; vocabulary)
- wasi.vocab.raw (WASI vocab raw score)
- wasi.matr.raw (WASI matrices raw score; nonverbal IQ)
- wasi.iq (WASI FSIQ-2)
- wj3.watt.raw (WJ-III word attack; decoding)
- wj3.wid.raw (WJ-III letter-word; decoding)
- wj3.oralcomp.raw (WJ-III oral comprehension)
- celf.rs.raw (CELF recalling sentences raw score)
- celf.fs.raw (CELF formulating sentences raw score)
- towre.w.ipm (TOWRE words items per minute; decoding)
- towre.nw.ipm (TOWRE nonwords items per minute; decoding)
- wj3.rf.ipm (WJ-III reading fluency items per minute; decoding)
- handedness
- structural MRI ID
- ktea2.raw (KTEA reading comp raw score)
- gm.rcomp.raw (Gates-McGinitie reading comp raw score)
- nd.rcomp.raw (Nelson-Denny reading comp raw score)
- wj3.rcomp.raw (WJ-III Passage comp raw score)

A195 and A197 are fully updated as of 9/27/2018.

A182 and A40-4 have not been fully updated.
